Mineral Waterksh 112.50

Mineral water comes from a spring and contains stable levels of minerals like calcium, magnesium, and sodium, with minimal treatment beyond filtration and disinfection.

Purified Waterksh 114.25

Purified water can come from various sources and is treated with processes like reverse osmosis or distillation to remove impurities, leaving it essentially free of minerals.
